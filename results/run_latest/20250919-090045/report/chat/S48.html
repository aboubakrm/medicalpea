Patient HR+/HER2- progressed after CDK4/6; what key label-based caveats should I discuss?
Dr Tawel
In this scenario, consider the patient’s performance status and any visceral disease. For HR+/HER2- after CDK4/6 progression, options may include endocrine therapies or chemotherapy, guided by the label. Please refer to the SmPC or EPAR for specific data and details.
